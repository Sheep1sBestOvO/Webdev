Potato Chips Recipe

These homemade potato chips are crispy, lightly salted, and a perfect snack for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 large russet potatoes
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Wash and slice the potatoes
  2. Soak the slices
  3. Dry thoroughly
  4. Heat the oil
  5. Fry in batches
  6. Drain and season
  7. Cool and serve
