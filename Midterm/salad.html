Fresh Garden Salad

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Carrots
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions

  1. Chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Mix them in a bowl.
  3. Drizzle olive oil and sprinkle salt & pepper.
  4. Gently toss everything together and serve fresh.
